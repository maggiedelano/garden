My Workflows

Last updated on August 6, 2021

This is the entry node to a set of notes on my workflows. The creation of these notes was inspired in part by an episode of the Deep Questions podcast by Cal Newport.1 While Cal suggests for the documents to be tool agnostic I do love my Tech Stack. I’ll try and keep the high level ideas separate from some of the implementation details so anyone can try them out without relying on a specific set of tools.
